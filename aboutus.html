About WeCare CBO

A youth-led organization strengthening accountability, transparency, and citizen participation through social accountability, policy advocacy, and public contract monitoring across Kenya.

Our Story

From Vision to Impact

WeCare Community Based Organization (WeCare CBO) was founded in 2021 with one clear mission: to strengthen accountability, promote public participation, and monitor public contracts to ensure transparency and integrity in governance.

Born from youth leaders who recognized gaps in citizen engagement, WeCare has grown into a practical, community-rooted force for democratic participation across Kenya.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission & Approach

Vision

A society inclusively involved in sustainable, transparent democratic and governance processes where every citizen has a meaningful voice.

Mission

To strengthen the capacity of citizens, especially youth, women, and persons with disabilities, to meaningfully participate in governance.

Approach

We collaborate with citizens, civil society, government agencies, and development partners to deliver practical accountability solutions.
