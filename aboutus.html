Our Story

From Vision to Impact

WeCare Community Based Organization (WeCare CBO) was founded in 2021 with one clear mission: to strengthen accountability, promote public participation, and monitor public contracts to ensure transparency and integrity in governance.

Born from youth leaders who recognized gaps in citizen engagement, WeCare has grown into a practical, community-rooted force for democratic participation across Kenya.

Operating primarily in Western Kenya (including Kisumu, Siaya, and Kakamega) with extended scope to Nairobi, we prioritize inclusive participation of youth, women, and persons with disabilities.

Our Foundation

The principles guiding our work toward transparent, accountable, and inclusive governance.

Vision

A society inclusively involved in sustainable, transparent democratic and governance processes where every citizen has a meaningful voice in shaping their future.

Mission

To strengthen the capacity of citizens, especially youth, women, and persons with disabilities, to meaningfully participate in governance and democratic processes.

Approach

We collaborate with citizens, civil society, government agencies, and development partners to deliver practical accountability and integrity solutions across Kenya.

Our Core Values

The values that shape our work with communities, partners, and stakeholders.

Integrity

Honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in everything we do.

Citizen-Centered

Citizens' needs and voices guide our priorities and actions.

Collaboration

Partnerships that create real, measurable collective impact.

Innovation
