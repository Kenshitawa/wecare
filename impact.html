How We Measure Impact

WeCare tracks results before, during, and after engagement sessions, combining community feedback with documented evidence and follow-up actions.

Our Evidence Approach

  • Pre/post session feedback from participants
  • Action plans with responsibilities and timelines
  • Follow-up tracking on commitments and resolutions
  • Documentation: attendance, photos, minutes, reports
  • Community validation of outcomes

Common Outcome Areas

  • Increased public participation and civic confidence
  • Better access to information on public projects
  • Improved responsiveness from duty bearers
  • Stronger youth leadership and community organizing
  • Reduced gaps in service delivery through follow-up
