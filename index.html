Our Work

Focus Areas

Our work is rooted in youth civic engagement and accountable leadership, with practical action across transparency, public participation, legislative engagement, and digital rights.

Youth Civic Engagement

Strengthening citizen demand by equipping young people to organize, engage institutions, and participate meaningfully in democratic processes.

Transparency & Anti-Corruption

Promoting integrity through anti-corruption advocacy, social accountability, citizen oversight, and transparent governance systems.

Public Participation & Legislative Engagement

Supporting communities to influence policy through structured participation, memoranda submission, and civic dialogue.

Ethical Leadership Monitoring

Monitoring and demanding ethical leadership by amplifying citizen voices and encouraging leaders to uphold integrity.

Digital Rights

Championing digital inclusion, online civic space, access to information, and protection of digital rights.

Annual Reports & Memoranda

Documenting our work through annual reports, policy memoranda, and civic submissions that strengthen transparency and advocacy.

Who We Are

WeCare Youth-Based Organization

WeCare Community Based Organization (WeCare CBO) is a youth-led initiative committed to strengthening transparency, accountability, and citizen participation in governance.

We work with young people, community members, and public institutions to build ethical leadership, deepen civic engagement, and promote responsive democratic processes across Kenya.
