Our Focus Areas

Building youth leadership and meaningful participation in county and national decision-making spaces.

Discover our organization

WeCare Youth-Based Organization

WeCare Community Based Organization (WeCare CBO) is a youth-led initiative working to strengthen accountability, promote public participation, and monitor public contracts to ensure transparency and integrity.

WeCare operates in Western Kenya (including Kisumu, Siaya, and Kakamega) with extended scope to Nairobi, prioritizing inclusive engagement of youth, women, and persons with disabilities.
