Our Work

Focus Areas

WeCare operates across multiple strategic areas to promote good governance, transparency, and citizen participation in democratic processes.

Citizen Demand & Oversight

Building youth leadership and meaningful participation in county and national decision-making spaces through capacity development and advocacy.

Transparency & Accountability

Promoting citizen oversight, social accountability mechanisms, and anti-corruption action in governance processes at all levels.

Public Contracts Monitoring

Tracking public projects and procurement processes to strengthen openness, value for money, and public trust in government.

Policy Advocacy

Engaging stakeholders through dialogues, memoranda, and evidence-based advocacy for progressive policy reforms and legislation.

Digital Rights & Civic Tech

Supporting access to information, digital rights protection, and innovations that improve citizen reporting and engagement.

Human Rights & Social Justice

Advancing inclusion in civic spaces with a focus on youth, women, and persons with disabilities in governance processes.

Who We Are

WeCare Youth-Based Organization

WeCare Community Based Organization (WeCare CBO) is a youth-led initiative committed to strengthening accountability, promoting public participation, and monitoring public contracts to ensure transparency and integrity in governance.

Operating primarily in Western Kenya (Kisumu, Siaya, and Kakamega) with extended reach to Nairobi, we prioritize inclusive engagement of marginalized groups including youth, women, and persons with disabilities.

  • Social Accountability
  • Public Participation
  • Policy Advocacy
  • Digital Rights
  • Contracts Monitoring
  • Youth Leadership

Our Vision

Our Mission

Our Impact

Driving youth-centered governance through community dialogues, policy engagement, and partnerships that advance transparent, responsive leadership.
