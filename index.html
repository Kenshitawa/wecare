Our Featured Programs

Youth for Children believes that young people, having experienced and overcome systemic challenges, are well-placed to cushion the pathway for children and ensure safer, more inclusive learning environments.

Child Protection & Safeguarding

Empowering children to know themselves, use their voices, and advocate for their rights through our comprehensive CPAS curriculum.

Youth Leadership Development

Virtual programs nurturing the next generation of leaders with public speaking and advocacy skills.

Inclusive Education

Creating learning environments where every child feels safe, valued, and empowered to reach their full potential.

Climate Action Tales

Storytelling-based program instilling environmental conservation skills in children for a sustainable future.

Children Rights Clubs

Establishing platforms in schools across Kenya where children can voice their concerns and advocate for their rights.

Empowering the Future

Virtual youth development program building leadership capacity and communication skills among young changemakers.

Discover Our Organisation

Empowering the Next Generation

Youth for Children (YFC) was founded in 2023 by Stephen Okoth and Joy Tadayo, who began as teaching assistants through Pacemaker International at Magadi Primary School. Through close interactions with learners, they discovered many children facing different forms of abuse, inspiring the creation of YFC.

Our Vision

A just and flourishing society where children are empowered to be agents of their own meaningful change, inspired by the leadership of youth.

Our Mission

To build a just and flourishing society through youth-led programs that strengthen inclusive education and empower children to respond to contemporary issues.

Our Impact

255 children reached in 2025 across 5 schools in 4 counties with 18 youth leaders trained and Children Rights Clubs established nationwide.
